About us
CANGZHOU RITIA PIPE-FITTINGS MANUFACTURE CO.,LTD.has specialized in all types of industrial pipes and pipe fittings since our beginning in 1993.We have a staff of 216,including 27 professional technicians,and our company covers an area of 36,000 square meters.In order to provide high quality,economically priced industrial tubes,flanges,and industrial pipe fittings,we have purchased testing equipment which is used for chemical analysis,water pressure testing,X-ray inspection,ultrasonic testing,eddy current inspection,tension testing,chemicaltesting,etc.We are also ISO9001:2000 approved.
We are located in Cangzhou City,Hebei Province,near a large sea port and other convenient transportation,Thus,the shipping fees for our customers are reduced.

Prdoducts
We primarily provide industrial pipe,industrial tube fittings,and industrial flange.Our products are strong and sturdy,highly resistant to corrosion,and are SGS and ABS certified.They are used in a wide range of industries and applications,like electric power,petrochemical,chemical,pharmaceutical,boiler,heating,shipbuilding,etc.Due to their high quality and low price,our industrial pipes,flanges,and pipe fittngs,are exported to Poland,Italy,India,Thailand,Korea,Brazil,Spain,Middle East and South America,and so on.
