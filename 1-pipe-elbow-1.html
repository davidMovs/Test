Elbow

Pipe elbows are mainly used for connecting two pipes or tubes,allowing a change of direction. We can provide short radius elbows and long radius elbows.Usually,pipe elbows are made of carbon steels,alloy steel,stainless steel or PVC,and more.They have high strength,good toughness and resistance to corrosion. Our have DIN standard, JIS Standard, American Standard ect.
